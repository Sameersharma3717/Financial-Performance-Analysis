# 💰 Financial Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Financial Performance Analysis** solution built using **SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI**. The objective was to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product performance, discount strategies, and regional trends to generate actionable business insights.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow—from data cleaning and SQL-based business analysis to Python exploratory data analysis (EDA), Excel dashboard development, and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

# 🎯 Executive Summary

## Business Problem

Organizations need to monitor revenue, profitability, customer segments, and product performance to maximize growth and profitability. This project analyzes financial data to identify key revenue drivers, profitable products, seasonal sales trends, and the impact of discount strategies on business performance.

## Key Insights

- Total Revenue reached **$118.73M** with a total **Profit of $16.89M**.
- Overall Profit Margin is **14.23%**.
- **Paseo** generated the highest revenue and profit.
- **Government** and **Small Business** segments contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Revenue peaked during **October**, indicating strong fourth-quarter demand.
- Low and Medium discount strategies generated the highest profits.

## Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing products such as Paseo.
- Expand sales efforts within Government and Small Business customer segments.
- Replicate successful fourth-quarter sales strategies during lower-performing months.
- Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability.
- Improve performance in lower-performing markets through targeted sales initiatives.

## Business Impact

This project enables stakeholders to monitor financial performance, evaluate product profitability, optimize pricing strategies, and support data-driven business decisions through interactive dashboards.

# 📚 Business Metrics Dictionary

| Metric | Formula | Business Meaning |
|---------|----------|------------------|
| Total Revenue | Sum(Sales) | Total company revenue |
| Total Profit | Sum(Profit) | Overall business profit |
| Profit Margin | Profit ÷ Revenue × 100 | Business profitability |
| Units Sold | Sum(Units Sold) | Sales volume |
| Revenue by Country | Revenue grouped by country | Regional performance |
| Revenue by Segment | Revenue grouped by customer segment | Customer contribution |
